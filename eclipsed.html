    Eclipsed

    Created for Moving Images at SFU.

    This short film is adapted from the short story "Eclipsed" by Robert Shuster. There were three members on our production team.

    I was in charge of finding and hiring the actors for the video as well as organize the shoots. I assumed the role of the director while shooting and was one of the two filmographers. I did the original rough editing of the video, and edited the sound and color graded the final video.
