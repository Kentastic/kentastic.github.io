    Magazine Spread

    Created for Spatial Design at SFU. I designed some artistic styled magazine spreads in Adobe Illustrator. I also shot the photos and touched them up in Photoshop.

    The concept was to analyze Alexey Brodovich's designs in Harper's Bazaar, learn the design prinicples and apply them to a magazine spread.
