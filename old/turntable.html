    Turntable and Visualizer

    Created for Introduction to Technological Systems at SFU

    and shown at SFU Surrey Open House 2011

    I researched and purchased the sensors and micro-controller and wired the physical device.

    I designed the circuitry and which uses an Arduino Uno micro-controller and multiple sensors.

    The turntable connected to a laptop where you could play songs and edit them with the physical interface. Depending on the mix and music being played the visualiztion changes. I did all the programming for the micro-controller and the visualiztion program.
