    3D Texturing

    Created for Animation at SFU. I created this scene of a house with a lemonade stand using Maya 2010. I used fur for the grass and procedural textures for all the objects except the sign and crate which were uv mapped. The lemonade was made with nDynamics.

    Created for Immersive environments at SFU. In a group of 2 I created a video game in the Unity 3D engine. I modeled and textured the main character. I modeled the character low poly and then created a higher poly model then created a normal map from that and applied to the lower poly model to create a better better looking model while keeping the character low poly.
