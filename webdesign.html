    iamagamer.ca

    Working at East Side Games I was given the opportunity to develop the website for the iamagamer game jam. Leading the development I worked with a co-worker to develop the site from a design over 2 weeks. We developed the site in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.     dragonupgame.com

    I implemented a microsite for one of East Side Games games. I took a design for the desktop website and designed the responsive design for tablet and mobile versions of the site and then took those designs developed the full website and accompanying support page in PHP, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

    From this project I improved many of my skills from design to structuring website markup. I also learned about developing a website and iterating on a design while developing and having the lead designer provide input on changes.
